## Design Context

### Users
Demo visitors and developers exploring on-device AI inference in the browser via WebGPU/WebNN. They are evaluating model performance, testing different backends, and demonstrating browser-based LLM capabilities.

### Brand Personality
Warm, approachable, technically confident. The interface should feel inviting and friendly while still conveying precision around performance data.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone:** Warm and approachable — soft, tinted neutrals instead of pure grays; friendly accent colors
- **Theme:** Light mode only
- **Anti-references:** Cold corporate dashboards, dark-mode-only dev tools, generic AI cyan/purple gradients
- **References:** Friendly SaaS products that balance warmth with data clarity (Linear, Vercel dashboard)

### Design Principles
1. **Warm neutrals over cold grays** — tint backgrounds and text toward warm hues for approachability
2. **Color with purpose** — every color communicates meaning (state, category, emphasis), not decoration
3. **Metric clarity** — performance badges use distinct, semantically meaningful colors that are easy to scan
4. **Inviting but not playful** — the product handles serious tasks; color adds warmth without being childish
5. **Consistent palette** — 2-3 accent colors used systematically, not scattered rainbow
